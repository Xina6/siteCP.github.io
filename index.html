Our story






We set off with the ambition of entrepreneurship and the desire to create something we lacked: a responsible and freshly prepared dessert. Specifically, with healthy ingredients, with a natural taste, without many E's and powders or frozen. A different sweet experience, but to which I wanted to have easy access.

Almost 3 years have passed, spent more than we would have thought in the workshop, measuring, mixing and even remaking recipes, so as to obtain the taste and quality we wanted.

I tested new compositions and cakes, some less known, others already in trend. We were lucky to have a handful of passionate confectioners and pastry chefs with us over time.

But our journey seasoned with cakes and artisanal cakes continues, with plans and ideas to create even more sweet experiences for those who choose to be our customers.

So, if you are looking for an artisanal dessert or eager to enjoy your guests with cakes and pastries, we would be happy to meet you.
